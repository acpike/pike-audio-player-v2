🎯 Recommended: Apple Music Vibrancy

For your space-themed music player, the Apple Music Vibrancy approach will give you that premium iOS feel users expect. The high saturation and brightness create luxury while the multiple shadow layers add sophisticated depth that separates beautifully from your dark track tiles.

Technical Implementation:

backdrop-filter: blur(40px) saturate(2.0) brightness(1.1)
background: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(255,255,255,0.35), rgba(255,255,255,0.25))
box-shadow: Multi-layered white glow + inset highlights
